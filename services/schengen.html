Document Requirements

Schengen visa applications require specific documentation. Here are the common documents needed:

  • Valid passport with blank pages
  • Recent Schengen-format photos
  • Flight reservations
  • Hotel bookings
  • Travel insurance
  • Bank statements
  • Employment letter
  • Income tax returns
  • Travel itinerary
  • Cover letter
  • Leave approval letter
  • Proof of accommodation

Note: Requirements may vary by country and visa type. Our consultants will provide a customized checklist.
