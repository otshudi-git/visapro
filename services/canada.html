Document Requirements

Canadian visa applications require specific documentation. Here are the common documents needed:

  • Valid passport
  • Digital photos meeting IRCC specifications
  • Completed application forms
  • Proof of financial support
  • Employment letters and pay stubs
  • Bank statements
  • Educational credentials
  • Language test results (IELTS/CELPIP)
  • Travel history
  • Proof of ties to home country
  • Medical examination results
  • Biometrics

Note: Requirements vary by visa type and personal circumstances. Our consultants will provide a customized checklist.
