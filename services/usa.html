Document Requirements

Every visa application requires specific documentation. Here are the common documents needed for USA visa applications:

  • Valid passport (valid for at least six months beyond your intended stay)
  • Recent photograph (2x2 inches, meeting US visa photo requirements)
  • DS-160 confirmation page
  • Visa fee payment receipt
  • Appointment confirmation letter
  • Travel itinerary (if available)
  • Accommodation details
  • Financial documents showing ability to fund your trip
  • Employment verification letter (if employed)
  • Educational documents (for student visas)
  • Tax returns and bank statements
  • Proof of ties to your home country

Note: Additional documents may be required based on your specific visa category and personal circumstances. Our consultants will provide you with a customized checklist.

US Visa Interview Tips

Be Punctual

Arrive at least 30 minutes before your scheduled interview time.

Dress Professionally

Make a positive first impression with business casual attire.

Be Concise

Give clear, direct answers to the consular officer's questions.

Organize Documents

Have your documents well-organized and easily accessible.

Be Honest

Always provide truthful information during your interview.

Demonstrate Ties

Be prepared to show strong ties to your home country.
